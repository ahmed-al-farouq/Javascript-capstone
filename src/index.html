Ahmed & Francis are remote pair-programmers and software development students of Microverse. Microverse is a software development programme that uses remote pair-programming and project building to teach its student development.

Ahmed is from Egypt and Francis is from Nigeria. They built this project during their Javascript Capstone week in Microverse, putting into practice the technical and soft skills learned during the course of the programme.
